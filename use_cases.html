Banking & Finance

Customer Identity Verification (KYC)

Banks deploying WhiteNet issue every customer a CA-signed cryptographic certificate bound to a unique IPv6 address. Before any transaction — fund transfer, loan application, account access — the customer's identity is verified in real-time against the WhiteNet trust engine.

  • ✔ Deepfake KYC fraud prevention — identity is cryptographic, not biometric-only
  • ✔ Real-time 7-factor trust scorecard before every transaction
  • ✔ TLS 1.3 encrypted session between customer and bank server
  • ✔ Tampered certificates are instantly detected and BLOCKED
  • ✔ Immutable audit trail — every verification event is logged and signed

Banking & Finance

Agent & Employee Verification

When a customer calls their bank, how do they know the person on the other end is a real bank employee and not a scammer? WhiteNet issues each agent a verified identity. Before connecting to a customer, the agent's certificate is verified — impersonators are immediately blocked.

  • ✔ Every bank agent holds a WhiteNet-verified certificate
  • ✔ Impersonators detected via CA signature mismatch
  • ✔ Customers can verify their agent is legitimate in real-time
  • ✔ Revoked employees are instantly blocked from the network
  • ✔ Governance proposals manage agent onboarding policies

E-Governance & National ID

Citizen Identity for Government Services

Government portals — tax filing, benefit claims, voting systems — are prime targets for identity fraud. WhiteNet ensures every citizen accessing a government service holds a verified digital identity cryptographically bound to their network address. Ghost beneficiaries and duplicate identities become impossible.

  • ✔ Eliminates ghost beneficiaries in welfare systems
  • ✔ DNSSEC-signed domain resolution for government portals
  • ✔ Citizen identity lifecycle: issue → verify → revoke → renew
  • ✔ Transparent governance — policy changes require community vote
  • ✔ Full audit trail for regulatory compliance

Healthcare

Medical Record Access Control

Patient records are among the most sensitive data in existence. WhiteNet ensures only verified healthcare professionals can access medical records — via TLS-secured channels with full identity verification. Unauthorized access attempts are blocked and logged.

  • ✔ Doctor/nurse identity verified before record access
  • ✔ TLS 1.3 encrypted channel (AES-256-GCM-SHA384)
  • ✔ Revoked credentials instantly block access
  • ✔ VPN tunnel for inter-hospital data exchange
  • ✔ Signed audit chain for HIPAA/GDPR compliance

Corporate Zero Trust

Employee Access & VPN Security

Traditional VPNs trust anyone with credentials. WhiteNet enforces Zero Trust at the identity level — employees must hold a valid, non-revoked WhiteNet certificate to establish a VPN tunnel. Terminated employees are instantly revoked; compromised credentials are detected via certificate signature verification.

  • ✔ Employee onboarding → WhiteNet certificate issuance
  • ✔ VPN tunnels require verified identity on both endpoints
  • ✔ Terminated employee → certificate revoked → access blocked
  • ✔ AES-256-GCM + HMAC-SHA384 encrypted tunnels
  • ✔ Trust posture assessment before granting access
